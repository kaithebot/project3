# Livre Magique - Operational Excellence Plan

## Quality Standards

### Book Production
**Print Quality Checklist:**
- [ ] Paper weight: 150gsm minimum
- [ ] Color accuracy: Pantone matching
- [ ] Binding: Perfect bound, durability test
- [ ] Packaging: Protective wrap + box
- [ ] Inspection rate: 100% for first 100 orders, then 20% sampling

**Defect Rate Target:** <1%
**Customer Complaint Resolution:** <24 hours

### AI Story Generation
**Quality Metrics:**
- Grammar check: 100% automated + 10% human review
- Personalization accuracy: 99.5%
- Cultural sensitivity review: Required for all stories
- Reading level appropriateness: Age-matched

## Fulfillment Process

### Order to Delivery Timeline
**Morocco:** 3-5 business days
**France:** 4-6 business days  
**Azerbaijan:** 5-7 business days

### Process Flow
1. **Order Received** (0-2 hours)
   - Payment confirmation
   - Data validation
   - Customer notification

2. **Story Generation** (2-6 hours)
   - AI creates personalized story
   - Human review (random sampling)
   - Customer preview sent

3. **Production** (1-2 days)
   - Print queue
   - Quality control
   - Packaging

4. **Shipping** (1-3 days)
   - Carrier pickup
   - Tracking notification
   - Delivery confirmation

## Risk Management

### Supply Chain Risks
**Printer capacity limitations**
- Mitigation: 2 backup printers, digital option

**Paper/material shortages**
- Mitigation: 30-day safety stock, multiple suppliers

**Shipping delays**
- Mitigation: Multiple carriers, clear communication

### Operational Risks
**Key person dependency**
- Mitigation: Cross-training, documentation, gradual hiring

**Quality issues**
- Mitigation: Inspection protocols, supplier audits, insurance

**Technology failures**
- Mitigation: Backups, redundancy, manual processes
