## A/B Testing Roadmap

### Test 1: Charm Pricing
- **Variant A:** €32.99
- **Variant B:** €33.00
- **Hypothesis:** .99 pricing increases conversion in FR market

### Test 2: Bundle Framing
- **Variant A:** "Buy 3 for €79.99 (Save €19)"
- **Variant B:** "3 books - 20% off"
- **Hypothesis:** Absolute savings more compelling than %

### Test 3: Shipping Presentation
- **Variant A:** €4.99 shipping
- **Variant B:** Free shipping (price embedded: €37.99)
- **Hypothesis:** Free shipping reduces cart abandonment
