# Livre Magique - France Go-To-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Target:** Launch personalized children's book service in France
**Timeline:** 90-day phased rollout
**Initial Investment:** €15,000-€25,000
**Revenue Target:** €50,000 first year

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

### 1.1 Legal & Administrative
- [ ] Register French business entity (SAS or SARL)
- [ ] Open French business bank account
- [ ] Obtain SIRET number
- [ ] Register for French VAT (TVA)
- [ ] GDPR compliance audit
- [ ] Cookie consent implementation

### 1.2 Website Localization
- [ ] Translate full website to French
- [ ] Set up French domain: livremagique.fr
- [ ] Implement French payment processors (Carte Bancaire, PayPal France)
- [ ] French shipping integration (La Poste, Chronopost)
- [ ] French customer service phone line

### 1.3 Content Creation
- [ ] French blog content (10 articles)
- [ ] French social media templates
- [ ] French email sequences (5 emails)
- [ ] French video content (3 videos)

## Phase 2: Pre-Launch (Days 31-60)

### 2.1 Marketing Setup
- [ ] Google Ads France account (€500/month budget)

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low Initial Sales
**Mitigation:** Start with small ad spend, scale only when ROAS > 2:1

### Risk: Shipping Issues
**Mitigation:** Partner with established French fulfillment center after 100 orders/month

### Risk: Cultural Misalignment
**Mitigation:** Hire French native speaker for customer service and content review

### Risk: Competition Response
**Mitigation:** Focus on unique personalization features and superior customer service
