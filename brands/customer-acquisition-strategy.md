# Livre Magique - Customer Acquisition Strategy

## Overview
**Goal:** Acquire 4,700 customers in 2026
**Budget:** €65,000
**Target CAC:** €8-13

## Channel Mix

### 1. Paid Social (40% of budget - €26,000)
**Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok

**Strategy:**
- Lookalike audiences from existing customers
- Retargeting website visitors
- Video ads showing book creation process
- Carousel ads with customer testimonials

**Metrics:**
- CPM: €8-12
- CTR: 2.5%
- Conversion rate: 3%
- CAC: €10

**Expected Customers:** 2,600

### 2. Influencer Marketing (25% - €16,250)
**Target:** Parenting influencers, mommy bloggers, book reviewers

**Tiers:**
- Micro (10k-50k): 20 influencers × €300 = €6,000
- Mid (50k-200k): 10 influencers × €800 = €8,000
- Macro (200k+): 2 influencers × €1,125 = €2,250

**Expected Customers:** 650
**CAC:** €25 (higher but builds brand)

### 3. SEO/Content (15% - €9,750)
**Content Types:**
- Blog posts ("Best personalized gifts for kids")
- Video content (unboxing, reviews)
- Guest posts on parenting sites

**Expected Customers:** 400
**CAC:** €24 (long-term play)

### 4. Email Marketing (10% - €6,500)
**Tactics:**
- Lead magnets (free story templates)
- Welcome sequences
- Abandoned cart recovery
- Re-engagement campaigns

**Expected Revenue from Email:** €45,000
**ROI:** 7x

### 5. Referral Program (10% - €6,500)
**Structure:**
- Referrer: €10 credit
- Referee: 15% off first order

**Expected Referrals:** 1,050 customers
**CAC:** €6.20
